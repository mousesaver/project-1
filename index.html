Instruction
  • Select a category
  • Put your and your opponent's names in the boxes.
  • To play against the computer, put "Computer" as Player 2 name. You can also add space and a number between 1 and 5 after computer to select the difficulty such as "Computer 3"
  • Click start to play
  • Take turns clicking squares to reveal emojis
  • Find emojis in the category to score points
  • When all squares are revealed, the player having the higher points is the winner
  • Click reset to restart a game
Follow the instruction and start the game!
